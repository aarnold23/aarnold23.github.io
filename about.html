About the Project

The Problem

Kampalaâ€™s urban growth has led to severe traffic congestion in areas like Nakasero Market, Kololo, and Kampala Road, with delays costing millions in fuel and productivity annually. Manual traffic light systems fail to adapt to varying vehicle densities.

Our Solution

Our Automated Traffic Light Management System uses a Raspberry Pi 4, Picamera2, and YOLOv5-nano to detect toy cars and adjust traffic light timings dynamically (5-20s based on car counts). This prototype, tested in a two-lane setup, is a foundation for scalable solutions at intersections like Wandegeya or Clock Tower.
