About the Project

Kampala’s Traffic Challenge

Kampala’s rapid urbanization has overwhelmed its road network, with intersections like Nakasero Market and Kololo losing an estimated 1.5 million hours annually to congestion, costing millions in fuel and emissions (KCCA, 2024). Traditional traffic lights lack adaptability, exacerbating delays for matatus, bodas, and private vehicles.

Our Innovative Solution

Developed by Group 40, our Automated Traffic Light Management System leverages a Raspberry Pi 4, Picamera2, and YOLOv5-nano to detect toy cars and dynamically adjust traffic light timings (5-20s based on vehicle density). This prototype, achieving 85% detection accuracy, is a scalable model for optimizing Kampala’s intersections like Wandegeya or City Square.
