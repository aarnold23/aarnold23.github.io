A Solution for Kampala's Traffic Crisis

Transforming urban mobility with intelligent traffic management

In 2025, Kampala's roads, such as Entebbe Road and Jinja Road, lose millions of hours annually to gridlock. Our Automated Traffic Light Management System, developed by Group 40, uses a Raspberry Pi 4 and YOLOv5-nano to detect vehicle density and optimize traffic light timings.

Tested with toy cars, this prototype achieves 85% detection accuracy and is poised to transform intersections like Wandegeya and Clock Tower, saving time and reducing emissions.

85% Accuracy

YOLOv5-nano detects toy cars with high precision using advanced computer vision algorithms.

Dynamic Control

Adjusts green light times (5-20s) based on real-time car counts and traffic density analysis.

Scalable Design

Ready for deployment across Kampala's busiest intersections with modular architecture.
