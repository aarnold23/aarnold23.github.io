A viable solution for traffic congestion in the city.

Transforming urban mobility with intelligent traffic management

Traffic congestion is a significant issue in urban settings, leading to delays, fuel waste, and environmental pollution. In big cities like Kampala, automated light control fails to adapt to varying vehicle densities at different times of the day. This project targets the challenge of creating an efficient, automated system to manage traffic lights, reducing wait times and optimizing flow, which is crucial for scalable urban traffic solutions.

Tested with toy cars, this prototype achieves 85% detection accuracy.

85% Accuracy

YOLOv5-nano detects toy cars with high precision using advanced computer vision algorithms.

Dynamic Control

Adjusts green light times assigning 3s per car based on real-time car counts.

Scalable Design

Can be deployed to traffic lights across Kampala.
