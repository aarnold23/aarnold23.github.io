A Solution for Kampala’s Traffic Crisis

In 2025, Kampala’s roads, such as Entebbe Road and Jinja Road, lose millions of hours annually to gridlock. Our Automated Traffic Light Management System, developed by Group 40, uses a Raspberry Pi 4 and YOLOv5-nano to detect vehicle density and optimize traffic light timings. Tested with toy cars, this prototype achieves 85% detection accuracy and is poised to transform intersections like Wandegeya and Clock Tower, saving time and reducing emissions.
