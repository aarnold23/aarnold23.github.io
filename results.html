Results and Impact

Performance Metrics

YOLOv5-nano achieved 85% accuracy in detecting toy cars after adjusting confidence thresholds to 0.3. The system dynamically adjusts green light durations (e.g., 10s for 3 cars, 15s for 5 cars). Data is logged for analysis in CSV format.

Kampala Impact

By reducing wait times at intersections like Mulago Roundabout, this system could save Kampala commuters 10-15 minutes daily and cut emissions.

Limitations

Camera latency (1-2s) due to YOLO processing and manual lane splitting limited real-time performance. Testing was constrained to a single Raspberry Pi setup.
